Endangered Species in Focus

Giant Pandas

Once hunted to near extinction, giant pandas are making a comeback through international conservation efforts and habitat protection in China.

Sea Turtles

Ocean pollution, fishing nets, and climate change threaten sea turtles. Protecting nesting beaches is crucial for their survival.

African Elephants

Poaching and habitat loss have decimated elephant populations. These intelligent giants need our protection and larger reserves.

Polar Bears

Melting ice due to climate change is destroying polar bear habitats. They depend on sea ice for survival and hunting.

Orangutans

Deforestation in Southeast Asia is destroying orangutan habitats. These intelligent primates need rainforest protection.

Blue Whales

The largest animals on Earth were hunted to near extinction. Ocean protection and sustainable fishing practices are vital.
